== Description ==

= WordPress Email Outreach  Plugin =

MailScout provides a gmail based email delivery service that assists businesses with email delivery. The service manages various types of email including shipping notifications, friend requests, sign-up confirmations, and email newsletters. Additionally, the company provides link tracking, open rate reporting.

== MailScout is useful for : ==

* Lead Generation
* Email outreach
* Influence Marketing
* Digital Marketing
* CPA (Cost per action) Marketing
* Affiliate Marketing
* Blog Outreach
* Email Newsletter
